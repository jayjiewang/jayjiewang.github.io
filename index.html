Biography

Jie Wang is now a Senior Researcher with Tencent, Shenzhen, China. He also has a close research collaboration with ARC Lab and AI Lab of Tencent. His current research interests lie in the areas of deep learning and multimodal learning, specially for video and language.

Jie Wang got his PhD from Department of Electrical Engineer, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. His Ph.D. research topics are Lidar 3D imaging and processing. Previously, he was a Senior Researcher with PingAn AI Lab, collaborating with Prof. Shaojun Wang and Dr. Jing Xiao in multimodal understanding and generation.

He is now hungry for oppotunities in autonomous driving and 3D sensing.
